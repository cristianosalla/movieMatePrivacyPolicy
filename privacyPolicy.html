Privacy Policy for Movie Matte

Last updated: May 5, 2025

Movie Matte (“the App”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle information within the app.

The App only collects minimal user information necessary for authentication:

Your email is used solely for login and account management within the app. We do not use your information for marketing, analytics, or tracking.

3. Third-Party Services

We use Firebase Authentication provided by Google to manage login functionality. Firebase may collect some technical information to ensure security and proper function. You can read more here:

  • Firebase Privacy & Security

Your login information is retained only as long as your account is active. You may delete your account at any time by contacting us.

5. Children’s Privacy

This app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
